Treat everyone with respect

Everyone deserves to be part of this group and has the right to enjoy the experience without fear of harassment or discrimination.

All participants of the London Salesforce Developers are required to abide by the following code of conduct.

Code of Conduct

The London Salesforce Developers are committed to providing a harassment-free and inclusive environment for everyone regardless of ethnicity, race, religion, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, political affiliation, disability, physical appearance, body size, or opinion on tabs versus spaces. We do not tolerate any behaviour that would lead to another participant feeling unsafe or concerned for their physical or emotional well-being.  

Unacceptable behaviour includes, but is not limited to:

  • Stalking/following
  • Deliberate intimidation
  • Unwanted photography or recording
  • Sustained disruption of talks/presentations
  • Offensive comments - verbal, written or posted on social media
  • Inappropriate or offensive jokes
  • Sexual imagery or language in public spaces
  • Inappropriate physical contact
  • Unwelcome sexual or physical attention
  • Spamming, trolling, baiting, flaming
  • Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behaviour

This Code of Conduct may be revised at any time.

Enforcement

Participants asked to stop any inappropriate behaviour are expected to comply immediately.

Any person violating these rules may be removed from the event and banned from future participation.

Reporting

If you experience harassment, see someone else being harassed, or have any concerns, please contact an organiser either in person or using the Contact Us button in the Group Leaders section of our Trailblazer Community Group homepage :

https://trailblazercommunitygroups.com/salesforce-developer-group-london-united-kingdom/

All reports will remain confidential.
